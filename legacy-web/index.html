What counts as a transaction?
Every expense, income entry, or transfer you log. The 100-transaction free limit is generous enough to try Pocket properly for 2–3 months.
What happens when the year ends?
You drop back to the Free tier. Your data is never deleted — you just won't be able to add new transactions beyond 100 until you renew.
Can I upgrade later?
Yes, anytime from Settings inside the app. Payments are processed securely via Razorpay. No auto-renewal without your confirmation.
Privacy-first

Your finances are nobody else's business

Pocket stores your data in your own browser and your own cloud account. No ads, no data selling, no tracking. Your AI key and receipt images are only ever sent to Google's API when you choose to scan — never to us.
